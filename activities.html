Activities

Scroll through our favorite sights, restaurants, and shopping locations to ensure you make the most out of your UMICH experience!

Sights

Nichols Arboretum

Destress with a walk in the Arb and explore the dozens of native and exotic trees and shrubs! Take a look at the river, have a picnic in the fields, or go on a run on the trail - the arb has got it all!

Law Quad

The law quad is a university campus square surrounded by amazing neo-Gothic buildings, with lawns & paved paths. The law quad is by the best study spot on campus on a nice day. Be sure to bring a blanket to lay out on and some snacks.

UMMA

Check out the University of Michigan Museum of Art in Ann Arbor, which is one of the largest university art museums in the United States! It contains more than 150 years of history, with over 20,000 works of art. And admission is free!

Museum of Natural History

The Museum of Natural History is located in one of UMICH's very own classrooms, the Biological Sciences Building. Check out the museums four major exhibits - The Hall of Evolution, The Michigan Wildlife Gallery, The Anthropology Displays, and The Geology Displays.

"The Docks"

Looking to cool down on a hot day? Visit Bandameer park and take a dip in the Huron River. It is the best place to relax, tan, and socialize with other UMICH students!

Restaurants

Zingerman's

You must stop by at Zingerman's in Kerrytown for an Ann Arbor classic lunch. Grab yourself a reuban, or head on over to the attached grocery store for some pre-made meals.

Jagged Fork

Jagged Fork is the most popular breakfast destination in AA by far. Be sure to bring your parents to a delicious breakfast or brunch of clever pancakes, sandwiches, and salads.

Savas

Enjoy an elevated brunch or dinner at Savas. Not only are all their food locally sources, they're delicious too! Order the bistro salad and the corn risotto to finish off an amazing meal.

Slurping Turtle

Enjoy a steaming hot bowl of ramen or a dragon roll at the best Japanese resturant in AA. They don't take reservations, so be prepared for a wait!

Shopping

State Street

Pitaya, Verbena, Bivouac, MDen, Target - State Street has all the stores you need to upgrade your wardrobe for the new year.

Kerry Town

Not only is Kerrytown known for its local shops and businesses, it also has a wonderful farmers market. From clothes to food, find yourself a special treat in Kerrytown.

Briarwood Mall

Only a 10 minutes drive from Central Campus, Briarwood mall has all you need for your back-to-school shopping. From Macy's to Urban Outfitters, Briarwood is set to give you a brand new wardrobe for the school year.

Main Street

Cherry Republic. Footprints. Fleet Feet. Shinola. Warby Parker. The list of stores on Main Street goes on and on.
